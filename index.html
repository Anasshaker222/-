FAQs

Answers to your top questions

Do I need technical experience to manage my site?
Not at all. We design intuitive dashboards and provide a comprehensive walkthrough during launch. We also remain available for future updates or technical questions.
How long does the design process take?
Typically between 3–7 days, depending on the scope of your project. We’ll give you a clear timeline during our initial consultation.
Can I update the website myself later?
Yes. We build on easy-to-use frameworks (with the Growth Plan) that allow you to manage products, images, and content without writing a single line of code.
What if I don't like the design?
We work collaboratively. You approve every stage of the design before development begins. If revisions are needed, we keep iterating until you're 100% satisfied.
