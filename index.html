Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to your top questions

Do I need technical experience to manage the store?
No. We design the store to be easy to use, and we walk you through everything after delivery. If you run into any issues, we're available to help.
How long does it take to design the store?
Usually 3 to 7 days — it depends on the number of products and the required details. We'll give you an exact timeline before we start.
Can I add products later?
Yes. The Starter Plan includes up to 15 products, and the Growth Plan has no limit. If you need adjustments, we're here for you.
What if I don't like the result?
We'll work with you to adjust it until you're satisfied. We never deliver something you haven't approved first.
